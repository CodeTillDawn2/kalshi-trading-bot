    BaseName

    Represents the Upside Gap Two Crows pattern, a three-candle bearish reversal pattern. Occurs in an uptrend with a bullish candle, followed by two bearish candles ("crows") where the second gaps up from the first and the third engulfs part of the second, closing below it but not too far from the first. Indicates a potential reversal from bullish to bearish momentum. Source: https://www.investopedia.com/terms/u/upside-gap-two-crows.asp

    ThirdCandleCloseTolerance

    Tolerance for how far the third candle s close can deviate above the first candle s close. Purpose: Ensures the third candle closes near the first to signal weakening momentum. Strictest: 0 (exact match), Loosest: 2.0 (allows wider deviation while still bearish).
