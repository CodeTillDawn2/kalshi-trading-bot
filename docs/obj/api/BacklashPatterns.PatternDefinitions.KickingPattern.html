    IsPattern(int, bool, int, Dictionary<int, CandleMetrics>, CandleMids[])

    Identifies a Kicking pattern, a two-candle reversal pattern. Requirements (sourced from Investopedia and other technical analysis resources):

    • Two large-bodied candles with opposite directions.
    • A gap between the candles (up for bullish, down for bearish).
    • Small or no wicks, resembling Marubozu candles.
    • Bullish Kicking follows a downtrend; Bearish Kicking follows an uptrend. Indicates: Strong reversal signal due to abrupt momentum shift.
