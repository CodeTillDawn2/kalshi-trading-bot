    Class InvertedHammerPattern

    The Inverted Hammer is a single-candle bullish reversal pattern after a downtrend.

    • Small body, long upper wick, minimal lower wick, bullish direction.
    • Indicates potential buying pressure and reversal to the upside. Source: https://www.investopedia.com/terms/i/invertedhammer.asp
