    IsPattern(int, int, Dictionary<int, CandleMetrics>, CandleMids[])

    Identifies a Piercing Pattern, a two-candle bullish reversal pattern. Requirements (source: Investopedia, BabyPips):

    • Occurs in a downtrend.
    • First candle is bearish with a significant body.
    • Second candle is bullish, opens below or near the first candle's close, and closes above the midpoint of the first candle s body but below its open. Indicates: Potential reversal from bearish to bullish momentum as buyers step in after a gap down.
