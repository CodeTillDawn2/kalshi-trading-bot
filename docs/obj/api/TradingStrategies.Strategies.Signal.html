    Enum Signal

    Defines the various trading signals used by the strategy system to identify market conditions and technical indicators. These signals represent different market states, technical analysis patterns, and external factors that can influence trading decisions. Each signal corresponds to a specific condition that can be evaluated against market data to determine appropriate trading actions.
