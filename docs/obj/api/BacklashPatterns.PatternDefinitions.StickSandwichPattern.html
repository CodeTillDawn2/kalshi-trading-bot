    IsPattern(int, bool, CandleMids[], Dictionary<int, CandleMetrics>, int)

    Identifies a Stick Sandwich pattern, a three-candle reversal pattern. Bullish: Occurs in a downtrend; two bearish candles sandwich a bullish candle, with c1 and c3 closes nearly equal. Bearish: Occurs in an uptrend; two bullish candles sandwich a bearish candle, with c1 and c3 closes nearly equal. Indicates a potential reversal as the middle candle s move is rejected. Source: https://www.tradingview.com/education/stick-sandwich/
