    IsPattern(Dictionary<int, CandleMetrics>, int, int, CandleMids[])

    Identifies a Rickshaw Man, a single-candle pattern similar to a Long-Legged Doji. Requirements (source: BabyPips, TradingView):

    • A small body (near-equal open and close) with long upper and lower wicks of roughly equal length.
    • Total range is significant, indicating volatility.
    • Close is near the candle�s midpoint. Indicates: Indecision in the market, often appearing at tops or bottoms, suggesting a potential reversal.
