    Class Snapshot

    Represents a comprehensive snapshot of market state and trading activity at a specific point in time. This entity captures detailed market data including pricing, volume, velocity metrics, and order book information for analysis, backtesting, and trading strategy evaluation. Snapshots serve as the primary data structure for understanding market behavior and making informed trading decisions.
