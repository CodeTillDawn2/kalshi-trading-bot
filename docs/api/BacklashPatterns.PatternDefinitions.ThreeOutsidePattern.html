    BaseName

    Represents a Three Outside pattern (Up or Down).

    • ThreeOutsideUp: A bullish reversal pattern after a downtrend. First candle is bearish, second candle is bullish and engulfs the first, third candle is bullish and confirms by closing higher.
    • ThreeOutsideDown: A bearish reversal pattern after an uptrend. First candle is bullish, second candle is bearish and engulfs the first, third candle is bearish and confirms by closing lower. Requirements sourced from: https://www.investopedia.com/terms/t/three-outside-up-down.asp
