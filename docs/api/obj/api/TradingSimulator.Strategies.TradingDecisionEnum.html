    Enum TradingDecisionEnum

    Defines the possible trading decisions that can be made by a trading strategy. This enum is used throughout the trading simulator to represent the core actions a strategy can recommend: purchasing an asset, maintaining the current position, or selling an existing position. It serves as the fundamental decision type for strategy evaluation and trade execution logic.

    Namespace: TradingSimulator.Strategies
    Assembly: TradingSimulator.dll
    Syntax
    public enum TradingDecisionEnum

    Fields

    Name Description
    Buy

    Indicates a recommendation to purchase or enter a long position in the asset. This decision is typically generated when strategy signals suggest upward price movement or favorable market conditions for buying.
    Hold

    Indicates a recommendation to maintain the current position without making changes. This decision is used when strategy signals do not strongly suggest buying or selling, or when the market conditions are neutral or uncertain.
    Sell

    Indicates a recommendation to sell or exit a long position in the asset. This decision is typically generated when strategy signals suggest downward price movement or unfavorable market conditions for holding the current position.
